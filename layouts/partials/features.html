Dynamic Match Creater 🛡️

Set up games with customizable parameters like field location, date/time, player count, and skill level. The system automatically handles waitlists and sends notifications when spots open up.

Field Finder 🚀

An interactive map showing nearby soccer fields with real-time availability, field conditions, lighting status, and field dimensions. Users can filter by features like turf/grass, indoor/outdoor, and post field reviews.

Skill Balancing 📈

Self-rate your skill level and preferred positions, allowing the app to automatically suggest balanced team compositions

Real-Time Availability 🦉

Mark your status as "available" or "busy" for an upcoming game

Payment Splitter ✨

Handle field rental fees, referee costs, or equipment expenses with integrated payment processing. Players can split costs evenly, set recurring payments for regular games, and track their payment history.

Auto-Translation 🧰

Automatically translate chats/messages into any lanugage of your choice
